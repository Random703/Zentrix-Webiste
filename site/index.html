The Problem

AIL and Globe Life agents face a very specific problem.

Leads go cold fast. A Will Kit request gets weaker by the day. An unanswered Globe Life card is money left on the table. A POS review that never gets booked is a policyholder drifting toward lapse.

But agents are already stretched thin with running presentations, submitting applications, managing their book. Prospecting calls are the first thing to slip.

That's the gap we were built to fill.

The Solution

We handle the outreach. You handle the presentation.

Zentrix is a dedicated appointment setting service built around AIL and Globe Life campaigns. Since 2022, we've worked union benefit leads, Will Kit follow-ups, Child Safety Kit responses, Globe Life direct mail and online leads, and POS review bookings. These five lead types drive most of the production for AIL and Globe Life agents across North America and New Zealand.

We don't need training on your product or your script. We know the AIL opening, the union benefit notification conversation. We know how to approach a Will Kit lead who returned their card two weeks ago. We know why a Globe Life response card prospect is a different conversation than a cold call. We've lived in these campaigns for three years.

Our service is simple: we work your leads so you don't have to. You show up to presentations. We fill your calendar.
