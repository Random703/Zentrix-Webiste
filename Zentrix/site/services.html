Services & Pricing

Simple, hourly pricing — only pay for the time we call.

Every plan is built around dedicated setters billed by the hour, starting from as little as $8/hour. No per-appointment surcharges, no hidden fees. Scale your calling hours up or down as your lead flow changes.

01 PAY-AS-YOU-GO

Pay-As-You-Go

From $8/ hour

For individual agents who want to get started with a single active lead campaign, paying only for the hours worked.

✓1 dedicated setter, billed hourly from as low as $8/hour, minimum 4 hours of calling per day, 5 days per week
✓Covers one lead type: Union AD&D, Will Kit, Child Safety Kit, Globe Life Response, or POS
✓Custom script aligned to your specific campaign and territory
✓Typically delivers 12–18 qualified, confirmed appointments per month
✓Direct calendar integration — GoHighLevel, Calendly, or Google Calendar
✓Lead list import and DNC scrubbing included
✓Weekly Monday report: dials, contacts, appointments set, no-shows rebooked
Best for: Individual AIL or Globe Life agents who want to try our service, build consistent appointment volume, and scale hours up or down.
Our commitment: We target 25 qualified appointments in your first 30 days of active calling. If we fall short, we add extra calling hours at no charge until that target is reached.
